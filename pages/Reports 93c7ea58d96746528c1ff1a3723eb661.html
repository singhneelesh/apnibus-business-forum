Overview

The "Cash Settlement" feature in the ApniBus Business app allows bus owners to settle their day to day Cash Hisaab from the bus conductor.

Widgets →

Pending Settlement Card

The Home Hisaab Card gives bus owners a quick overview of their daily earnings, booked seats, and a weekly comparison, making it easy to track key financial details at a single point.

  • Date: Displays the current date for quick reference.
  • Total Amount: Shows the total revenue generated for the selected day.
  • Seats Booked: Indicates the total number of seats booked on the selected day.
  • Last Week Summary: Provides a summary of revenue and bookings for the same day of the week in the last week.
  • Cash and Online Transaction Amount: Separates and displays the amount received in cash and through online transactions.

  • Why pending settlement amount card showing such a big amount?

  • How to view “Settled/Non-Settled Hisaab” of any “ specific date” ?

    1. In Hisaab Page, the Hisaab data is presented bus wise!
      1. The default selected date is Today
      1. You can see Yesterday Hisaab by tapping the toggle button at top to Yesterday!

    1. To look Hisaab of any specific date, Tap the Calendar icon at top right of application.

    1. Date Selection windows pops up, select any date from here & submit to view that day Hisaab.

  • How to view “Settlement Amount Details” of any trip?
    1. To open Hisaab Page, click ”Hisaab Dekhe >” button in Recent Trips Card or by tapping any of the “Hisab” cards.

    1. In Hisaab Page, the Hisaab data is presented bus wise, tap on any card to view its Trip Level Hisaab.

    1. To “Trip Hisaab” page show the Hisaab of Trips covered by that bus.

  • How to view “ Settle Trip Hisaab ”?
    1. To open Hisaab Page, click ”Hisaab Dekhe >” button in Recent Trips Card or by tapping any of the “Hisab” cards.

    1. In Hisaab Page, the Hisaab data is presented bus wise, tap on any card to view its Trip Level Hisaab.

    1. To “Trip Hisaab” page show the Hisaab of Trips covered by that bus.

    1. Go to “Trip Hisaab Details” page by tapping any of the Trip Cards.

    1. In the details page, there is a “Delete Button Icon” on top right side.

    1. In the details page, there is a “Delete Button Icon” on top right side.

    1. A dialog will pop-up with confirmation, click “Delete >” to delete the trip.
