Overview

The “Online Ticket” feature shows the tickets booked through both the POS machine and ApniBus Application.

Widgets →

Online Tickets Card

The Online Tickets card provides an overview of the route, bus number, price payable to the operator, seat category, booking time, and travel status.

  • Route: Displays the information about the departure point and destination of the bus journey.
  • Bus Number: Identify your ride with ease.
  • Price: Shows the ticket price which is payable to the operator.
  • Seat Category: Displays the seat categories available for a specific bus.
  • Booking Time: Display the booking time when bus seat was secured.
  • Travel Status: Get trip updates whether the journey is completed or ongoing.

FAQs →

  • Why my online booking is showing nothing on the screen?
    • The booking data might not have synced yet, ask to wait to atleast 30 mins.
    • May no booking have occurred through ApniBus Application and POS Machine.
    • If the issue is still not resolved, communicate the details with the team.

Tutorials →

  • How to view “Detailed Information” of a “specific booking”?
    • To open Online Booking Page, click ”View Details >” button in Online Tickets Card or by tapping anywhere on the “Online Tickets” card.

    • To look Detailed Information of any specific booking, Tap on the card.

    `

  • How to view “POS” category booking data?
    • To open Online Booking Page, click ”View Details >” button in Online Tickets Card or by tapping anywhere on the “Online Tickets” card.

    • In Online Booking Page, the Online Booking data is on ApniBus category!
      1. The default selected category is ApniBus.
      1. You can see POS Data by tapping the toggle button at top to POS!

  • How to view “Online Booking Data” of specific time period?
    • To open Online Booking Page, click ”View Details >” button in Online Tickets Card or by tapping anywhere on the “Online Tickets” card.

    • To look Online Booking of specific time period, Tap the Calendar icon at top right of application.
    • Date Picker Window Pops Up
    • Choose dates by clicking on the Date Selection window, pick both 'From' and 'To' dates, and then press the Submit button to view the Online Booking for that selected time frame.

  • How to share “Booking Details”?
    • To open Online Booking Page, click ”View Details >” button in Online Tickets Card or by tapping anywhere on the “Online Tickets” card.

    • To share the detailed information of the ticket click on the “Share” button.

    • Choose how you want to share, like messaging or social media. Share with friends!
  • How to “add contact” in Online Booking Card?
    • To open Online Booking Page, click ”View Details >” button in Online Tickets Card or by tapping anywhere on the “Online Tickets” card.

    • Press add contact button on the card

    • Enter Number windows pops up, now enter contact and press “Save >” to save Contact Number.
