Overview

The "Reports" feature in the ApniBus Business app allows bus owners to generate various reports for their businesses, such as,

  • Income Expense Report
  • Stage Wise Earnings
  • Stage Wise Details

and the user can also see all the previous generated reports as well.

FAQs →

  • Why Report card is showing on the list page, but when I open it, it shows message to , wait as the waybill report is being generated?
    • The primary reason for this is that the report generation happens on our side, and is processed in batches to maintain operability.
    • So when you generate a report it gets queued in our system and it generally takes 10-15 minutes to generate your report.
    • If it is taking any longer than that, please wait for 30 mins and if problem still persists contact support.
  • When clicked on ‘Generate Waybill’ button, it shows error message referring waybill cant be generated for the selected bus, What does it mean?
    • This generally means that you’re trying to generate reports for a bus that doesn’t have any POS Machine associated with it, thus resulting in error.
    • The reports can only be generated for buses that have POS connected.

Tutorials →

  • How to ‘View’ Reports?
    1. To open Reports, open side drawer from Home, then click ”Reports”.
    1. In Reports Page, there is no default selected bus, and shows the reports for all the buses.

    1. To look for any specific report click the button corresponding to it.
  • How to ‘View’ Reports of any “specific bus” ?
    1. To open Reports, open side drawer from Home, then click ”Reports”.
    1. In Reports Page, there is no default selected bus, and shows the reports for all the buses.

    1. To look for report specific to any single bus, click the “All Buses” drop down on top of the page.

    1. Bus Selection windows pops up, select any bus from here & submit to view that bus reports.
    1. Now the reports will be shown for the selected bus.
    1. To look for any specific report click the button corresponding to it.
  • How to ‘Download & Share’ any report?
    1. To open Reports, open side drawer from Home, then click ”Reports”.
    1. In Reports Page, there is no default selected bus, and shows the reports for all the buses.

    1. To look for any specific report click the button corresponding to it. Now click the download button at the bottom right of the screen.
    1. Storage permission will be requested to save the file to your local storage. Click ‘Allow” to download the report.
    1. After you allow the permission, the file be saved to your download folder, and a download will appear confirming the same.
    1. If you want to share the download file to any app, you can click the share button in the download confirmation dialog.
    1. The user device default share window will pop-up and user can select any app to share the file.
  • How to ‘Delete’ any report?
    1. To open Reports, open side drawer from Home, then click ”Reports”.
    1. In Reports Page, there is no default selected bus, and shows the reports for all the buses.

    1. To delete any report, click the delete button corresponding to it.
    1. Now confirm the Deletion on the pop up dialog.
