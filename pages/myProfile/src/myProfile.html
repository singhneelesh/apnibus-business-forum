  • Name: User’s full name.

  • Contact Details: User’s Contact information such as phone number.

FAQs →

  • My KYC is completed but why it is not reflecting on the screen?
    • The KYC might not have synced yet, ask to wait to at least 30 mins.
    • If the issue is still not resolved, communicate the details with the team.

Tutorials →

  • How to open “My Profile Page”?
    • To open “My Profile Page” click side Drawer button on the top left of the application.

    • Press “My Profile & KYC”
  • How to “Delete” profile?
    • To open “My Profile Page” click side Drawer button on the top left of the application.

    • Press “My Profile & KYC”
    • Press “Delete” icon on the top right of the screen.
    • Press “Delete Account” button.
    • Click “Ok” button to confirm the deletion of account.
