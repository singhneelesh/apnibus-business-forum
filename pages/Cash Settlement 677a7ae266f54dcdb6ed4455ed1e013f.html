Overview

The "Cash Settlement" feature in the ApniBus Business app allows bus owners to settle their day to day Cash Hisaab from the bus conductor.

Widgets →

Pending Settlement Card

This gives bus owners a quick overview of their total unsettled amount with their bus conductor i.e. the amount of cash they’ll receive from the bus conductors.

  • It is advised to Settle Trips after receiving the cash amount from the conductor or timely, so that the total sum stays right and data becomes helpful.
  • Settlement is pending: Shows the total cash amount left to be settled.
  • Last Settled On: Indicates the last day any amount was settled by you.
  • Trips: Provides the total number of trips that are not being settled.
  • Buses: The number of buses that comprises this data.

FAQs →

  • Why pending settlement amount card showing such a big amount?
    • The reason for this might be that you’ve not settled any or a lot of your past trips.
    • This results in the non-settled sum to be higher than expected, as it contains that you settled but haven’t marked as settled.
  • What happens if I click “Settle Trip” button on any trip?
    • That particular trip gets marked as settled by you.
    • That means, you’ve acknowledged that you’ve received the cash amount for the trip from the bus conductor, and all settlement is done by your end.

Tutorials →

  • How to view “Settled/Non-Settled Hisaab” of any “specific date”?
    1. To open Cash Settlement Page, open side drawer from home, then click ”Conductor Cash Settlement”.
    1. In Cash Settlement Page, the default selected date is Today

    1. To look Cash Settlement of any specific date, Tap the Calendar icon at top right of application.

    1. Date Selection windows pops up, select any date from here & submit to view that day Hisaab.
  • How to view “Settled/Non-Settled Hisaab” of any “specific bus”?
    1. To open Cash Settlement Page, open side drawer from home, then click ”Conductor Cash Settlement”.
    1. In Cash Settlement Page, the default selected date is Today

    1. To look Cash Settlement of any specific date, Tap the Calendar icon at top right of application.

    1. Bus Selection windows pops up, select any bus from here & submit to view that day Hisaab.
  • How to view “Settlement Amount Details” of any trip?
    1. To open Cash Settlement Page, open side drawer from home, then click ”Conductor Cash Settlement”.

    1. In Cash Settlement Page, the settlement data is presented in list form, tap on any card to view its Tickets level info.

    1. In “Cash Settlement Details” page show the Tickets of that trip.
      1. This view is in concise form of total tickets list
      1. It is grouped by tickets having same start & end destination

      c. When expanded, it shows all the tickets.

    1. To open Cash Settlement Page, open side drawer from home, then click ”Conductor Cash Settlement”.

    1. In Cash Settlement Page, the settlement data is presented in list form, tap on any card to view its Tickets level info.

    1. In “Cash Settlement Details” page, click the “Settle Trip >” button, it then show the Confirmation dialog, confirming which will settle the trip.
