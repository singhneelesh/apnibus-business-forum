Overview

"This feature on the recharge page allows users to effortlessly view their active subscriptions, pending payments, and recharge history.

Widgets →

Recharge - Total Payment Due Card

The Recharge-Total Payment Due Card is like a summary to help users keep track of money matters.

  • Date: Displays the current date for quick reference.
  • Pending Invoices: Indicates the total number of invoices pending to be paid.
  • Last Payment Due Date: Displays the last date by which the payment should be made.
  • Total Amount Due: Shows the total amount that needs to be paid.
  • Pay Now Button: A button that allows users to make the payment directly.
Individual Invoice Card

This individual invoice card offers a detailed view of each specific payment, making it easy for users to understand and manage their payment commitment.

  • Title: The title of the invoice, giving a quick idea of what the payment is for.
  • Subtitle: Additional details or a brief description to provide context to the invoice.
  • Summary/Description: A bit more information or a summary of the items/services covered by the invoice.
  • Amount: The base amount of the invoice, showing the cost of the items or services.
  • GST (Goods and Services Tax): If applicable, display the GST amount separately.
  • Total Amount: The grand total, combining the base amount and any applicable taxes.
  • Last Payment Due Date: The deadline for making the payment, ensuring timely processing.
  • Pay Now Button: A user-friendly button to initiate the payment process directly from the card.
Subscription Card

This card design allows users to easily comprehend the key details of their subscriptions, including payment information and upcoming expiry dates.

  • Subscription Title: The name or title of the subscription plan.
  • Expiry Date: The date when the current subscription period ends.
  • Subtitle: Additional information or a brief description related to the subscription.
  • Start Payment (Downpayment): Indicates the initial amount paid at the beginning of the subscription.
  • Online Booking Deduction: Specifies the amount deducted from online bookings, providing transparency on the deduction process.
  • Yearly Recharge Amount: Displays the total amount required for the yearly recharge of the subscription.
Recharge History Card

The Recharge History Page gives you a snapshot of your past transactions. Explore each card for details like title, date, payment method, and a breakdown of costs, providing a clear overview of your payment history.

  • Invoice Title: The title or name associated with the recharge transaction.
  • Invoice Date: The date when the recharge transaction took place.
  • Invoice Subtitle: Additional details or a brief descriptor related to the recharge.
  • Invoice Description: A summary or description of the items or services covered by the recharge.
  • Amount (GST Total): The total amount, inclusive of any applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST).
  • Payment Date: Indicates the date when the payment was made.
  • Payment Method (Digital or Cash): Specifies whether the payment was made digitally or in cash.

  • Why payment isn’t reflecting even if I have made the payment ?
    • Payment data might not have synced yet, wait at least 30 min for data to reflect.
  • My payment is failed but the amount is deducted ?
    • Deducted amount will be refunded within 5-7 days.
