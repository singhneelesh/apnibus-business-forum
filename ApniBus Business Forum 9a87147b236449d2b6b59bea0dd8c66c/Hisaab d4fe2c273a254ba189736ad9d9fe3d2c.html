Overview

The "Hisaab" feature in the ApniBus Business app allows bus owners to monitor and manage their hisaab effectively.

Widgets ‚Üí

Home Hisaab Card

The Home Hisaab Card gives bus owners a quick overview of their daily earnings, booked seats, and a weekly comparison, making it easy to track key financial details at a single point.

  • Date: Displays the current date for quick reference.
  • Total Amount: Shows the total revenue generated for the selected day.
  • Seats Booked: Indicates the total number of seats booked on the selected day.
  • Last Week Summary: Provides a summary of revenue and bookings for the same day of the week in the last week in form of percentage.
  • Cash and Online Transaction Amount: Separates and displays the amount received in cash and through online transactions and increase percentage of them from last week.
Top Performer Card

The Home Hisaab Card gives bus owners a quick overview of their daily earnings, booked seats, and a weekly comparison, making it easy to track key financial details at a single point.

  • Bus-Wise: Displays the top performing (top earning) buses of the selected day.
  • Route-Wise: Displays the top performing (top earning) routes of the selected day.
  • Amount: Shows the total revenue generated for the selected day along with its increase/decrease compared to last week.
  • Seats Booked: Indicates the total number of seats booked on the selected day.
